About Nexora

Founded by engineers who were tired of 3am pages.

Nexora started in 2018 as a two-person consultancy fixing broken deploy pipelines. Today we're a team of infrastructure engineers who believe reliability is a design decision, not an accident.

Our origin

Why we exist

Our founders spent a decade getting paged at 3am for deploys that should have taken thirty seconds. Nexora exists so other engineering teams don't have to.

How we work

Small teams, direct access

No layers of account management. You work with the same two or three engineers from kickoff to handover, and they stay reachable after launch.

What we believe

Four principles that shape every engagement

Reliability is a feature

Uptime isn't ops' problem to solve after launch — it's designed in from the first architecture diagram.

Automate the repeatable

If your team does it more than twice by hand, it belongs in a pipeline.

Visibility over guesswork

You should never have to ask "is it down?" — the dashboard already told you.

Hand over, don't lock in

We document and train so your team owns the system we build — not the other way around.
